Book a vacation to Maldives

Maldives - the perfect holiday destinationMaldives is a mesmerising blend of colours with its pristine white beaches, emerald green waters and dazzling blue skies. The perfect holiday destination for a romantic honeymoon or a leisure getaway from work. More...

What to do in MaldivesDid you know that Maldives is the flattest country in the world? So while there are no mountains to trek on, you can be assured of plenty of sun, sand and sea on a holiday to Maldives. A Maldives holiday is a great a choice for fun activities like scuba diving and island explorations. The island destination has something for everyone, making it great for a honeymoon package or a family holiday. Nature lovers can take a hike among the beaches and forests or go on a boating trip. Like some luxury? Relax with the best spa's in the world or just take a sunbath. Maldives also boasts of the world's first undersea restaurant where you can dine with the sharks! Make sure you don't miss out on the opportunity to swim with the gentle whale sharks since Maldives is one of the few places where you can find them.

Best time to goThe best time for a Maldivian holiday package would be November to April and December to March. What are you waiting for?
